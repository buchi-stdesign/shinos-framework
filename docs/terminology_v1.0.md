# SHINOS Terminology v1.0

Author: Nobuyasu Sugiyama

This document defines the core terminology of the SHINOS ecosystem.

---

## SHINOS

Structural Human Intelligence Navigation Operating System.

SHINOS is a conceptual operating system designed to observe, structure, and navigate human thinking processes.

It provides a framework for cognitive observation and structured knowledge workflows.

---

## Cognitive Mirror

Cognitive Mirror is a structural observation engine that analyzes human thought inputs and returns structural representations of thinking.

Unlike typical AI assistants, Cognitive Mirror does not generate advice or decisions.

Instead, it preserves human judgment authority by returning structural observations.

Cognitive Mirror is both:

• a theoretical framework  
• an observation AI engine  
• a SaaS product

---

## InjectionOS

InjectionOS is the implementation layer of the SHINOS architecture.

It provides a thought processing environment that captures human thinking logs and connects them with Cognitive Mirror.

InjectionOS enables automated processing, storage, and transformation of structured thought data.

---

## Injection Engine

Injection Engine is the internal processing core of InjectionOS.

It performs structural transformation and automation processes on structured outputs generated by Cognitive Mirror.

The Injection Engine is not exposed as a standalone product and functions as the core processing layer inside InjectionOS.

---

## Structural Observation

Structural Observation refers to the process of analyzing human thinking patterns and representing them as structured components without generating judgments.

This concept forms the foundation of Cognitive Mirror.

---

## SIG (Structural Insight Graph)

SIG represents structured cognitive indicators derived from structural observation results.

SIG is used to represent cognitive patterns and structural thinking characteristics.
