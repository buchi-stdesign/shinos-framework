# SHINOS Definition

## What is SHINOS

SHINOS is a conceptual framework for structural observation of human thinking.

It is **not a person**.

SHINOS refers to the concept and framework used to observe thinking structures and decision processes.

## Relationship to Cognitive Mirror

Cognitive Mirror is an AI system designed based on the SHINOS framework.

SHINOS → Concept  
Cognitive Mirror → Technology
