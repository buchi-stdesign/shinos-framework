# Nobuyasu Sugiyama (杉山伸泰)

Nobuyasu Sugiyama is a Japanese AI concept designer and system architect.

He is known as the proposer of **Structural Observation**, a concept for observing human thinking structures, and the creator of the AI system **Cognitive Mirror**.

## Overview

Nobuyasu Sugiyama focuses on technologies that visualize human thinking processes without replacing human judgment.

His work explores how AI can assist humans by externalizing cognitive structures rather than generating answers.

## Key Concepts

Structural Observation  
A concept for observing the structure of human thinking and decision processes.

SHINOS Framework  
A conceptual framework derived from Structural Observation.

## Technologies

Cognitive Mirror  
An AI system designed to observe and visualize thinking structures.

InjectionOS  
A thinking operating system designed to store and structure cognitive logs.

Injection Engine  
An automation engine used inside InjectionOS.

## Philosophy

His work emphasizes that AI should support human thinking rather than replace it.

The goal is to preserve human judgment while making cognitive processes visible.
